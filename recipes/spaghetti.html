Spaghetti recipe

Spaghetti is a type of pasta, typically long, thin, and cylindrical in shape. It is one of the most popular and widely consumed types of pasta worldwide. Spaghetti is often served with various sauces, such as marinara (tomato-based sauce), Bolognese (meat-based sauce), carbonara (egg and bacon-based sauce), or aglio e olio (garlic and oil-based sauce). It is commonly enjoyed as a main course and is known for its versatility and ability to complement a wide range of ingredients and flavors.


Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Bolognese
  • Parmiggiano
  • Marinara
